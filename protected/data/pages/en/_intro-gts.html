Working Groups

CEGOV's Working Groups (WGs) are responsible for the development of research projects, teaching and extracurricular activities, fellowship opportunities, and agreements with public and private institutions. Each GT is formed from a proposal signed by three or more professors from UFRGS, linked to two or more Departments. Research, teaching and/or extracurricular activities are previously approved by the Scientific Board. The WGs can also be integrated by both professors and students from other institutions, public officials, and political leaders.
