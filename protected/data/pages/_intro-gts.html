Presentation

The Working Groups (WGs) of CEGOV are responsible for the development of research projects, education and extension activities, and academic exchange agreements with public or private entities. Each GTs is formed from three or more proposition UFRGS teachers, linked to two or more Departments, constituted around the project (s) of research, teaching and / or extension approved (s) by the Scientific Council of CEGOV. Integrate the GTs, and teachers UFRGS, students and staff of the University, faculty and researchers from other institutions of education and research, members of government agencies, political leaders and associations.
